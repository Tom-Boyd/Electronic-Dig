by H. Trawick Ward

Feature 40 was a small, shallow basin measuring 1.3 ft by 1.0 ft by 0.2 ft deep. It was centered at 318.5R33.5. Fill consisted of a slightly mottled orange and brown loam with small amounts of charcoal. The sides sloped inward creating a basin-shaped profile. This feature was located inside Structure 4 and appears to be associated with it.
