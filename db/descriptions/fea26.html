by H. Trawick Ward

This shallow, circular basin measured 2.3 ft in diameter and was 0.6 ft deep. It was located near the center of the palisaded village at 252.2R48.5. The fill consisted of a sandy, bright yellow soil that was homogeneous and sterile except for small flecks of charcoal. The pit walls sloped inward at the bottom to create a conical profile.
