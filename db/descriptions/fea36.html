by H. Trawick Ward

Feature 36 was a small, irregular, cob-filled pit measuring 1.8 ft by 0.9 ft in plan view and 0.3 ft in depth. Fill consisted of a mottled orange-and-brown loam with charcoal and charred cob fragments. The profile was that of a shallow basin. Feature 36 was located within Structure 6 in a line between Feature 35 and Feature 37.
